Wizard101 is an MMORPG designed for all ages.
The first World you start your journey in as a young wizard is Wizard City; Ravenwood is the first zone you encounter. Upon entering the expansive and immersive experience, you are immediately tasked with defeating evil foes that stain the world, and are eventually tasked with purging the entire Spiral of all its malevolent harbingers.




Ravenwood can be described as a hub for all wizards. It offers schools where each player is able to train spells for their respective schools, in which they harness their powers to use. From there, each player is able to specialize in one of seven available schools: Death, Myth, Life, Balance, Storm, Ice, or Fire.
Whether you choose Theurgy or Conjuration, each school has its own unique charms that appeal to vast audiences.
